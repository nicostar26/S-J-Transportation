About S&J Transportation

S&J Transportation is a family owned and operated transportation company. Located in Raeford, NC we take pride in delivering your goods on time and in the same condition received. Our drivers are careful and considerate of your time and the quality of your goods. We take great pride in being one of the only small transportation companies who deliver both locally and nationwide.

We would love to take care of your transportation needs! Contact us using one of the methods below and we'll be happy to speak with you about how we better serve you!

About Our Drivers

When S&J Transportation was started in 2017, two brothers had a vision to build a company that drivers would love to work for. We believe that happy drivers make happy customers!

Our drivers are home every week (with the exception of new drivers who are home every other week) to spend time with their families and enjoy great pay.
